Ruffielart is a market place for the Arts and Crafts, Collectibles and Art materials. Our priority is to create many unique moments for artists to sign up, create their own gallery and activate sales of their Artworks to the world. We are actively involved in the African art scene through participation in Trade shows, Exhibitions, Periodical seminars and Educational training in order to maintain a foothold within the arts and crafts community and close relationships to customers.
